## Codebase Patterns
- All services in apps/web/src/services/ are registered globally in main.tsx via `register()`
- Use `this.resolve(ServiceClass)` in a getter to access other global Services from within a Service
- `HistoryData` and `ForecastData` in the PRD correspond to `StudyHistoryDayDto[]` and `ForecastDayDto[]` from `@echoe/dto`
- echoeRevlog.id is bigint (Unix ms √ó 1000); convert to Date via `new Date(Number(id) / 1000)`
- Stats service uses UTC dates: `today.setUTCHours(0, 0, 0, 0)` for day boundaries
- No user filtering in stats service (single-user system)
- Use `gte(echoeRevlog.id, timestamp * 1000)` for time-range queries on revlog
