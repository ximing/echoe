# Flashcards FSRS Full Chain - Progress

## Codebase Patterns
- Deck hierarchy uses '::' separator (e.g., "Parent::Child") to denote parent-child relationships
- Use `buildDeckHierarchy()` in echoe-deck.service.ts to convert flat deck list to tree structure
- FSRS stats are calculated per-deck in SQL queries, then aggregated in the service layer
- For recursive aggregation: newCount/learnCount/reviewCount/totalCount/matureCount/difficultCount are summed; averageRetrievability is weighted by totalCount; lastStudiedAt is MAX
